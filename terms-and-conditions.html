2. Services Offered

Joel B. Gacho provides the following professional freelance services:

  • WordPress Web Design — custom website design and development using WordPress and Elementor Pro, including responsive layouts, theme customisation, and plugin configuration.
  • SEO Services — on-page SEO optimisation, keyword research, meta tag implementation, site speed optimisation, and technical SEO audits.

Any work that falls outside the agreed scope is considered a change request and will be quoted separately. Joel B. Gacho reserves the right to decline scope changes that are incompatible with the original agreement or timeline.

4. Payment Terms

The following payment terms apply to all projects unless otherwise agreed in writing:

  • Deposit: A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total project fee is required before any work begins. This secures your spot in the project schedule.
  • Balance: The remaining 50% is due upon project completion, prior to the website going live or final files being delivered.
  • Ongoing retainers: Monthly SEO or maintenance retainers are invoiced at the start of each billing period and are due within 7 days.
  • Late payments: Invoices unpaid after 14 days may result in suspension of work or access to deliverables until payment is received.

Accepted payment methods include bank transfer, PayPal, and Wise (TransferWise). All prices are quoted in USD unless otherwise agreed.
