The Opportunity

Why Local SEO Matters Right Now

Nearly half of all Google searches carry local intent, and “near me” searches have grown dramatically over the past two years. For small businesses, appearing in the Google Maps 3-Pack and local search results is often the most direct path to new customers, more so than broad national rankings.

I recently went through the full Google Business Profile setup process for my own business and documented the entire process — see my Local SEO Bacolod guide for the exact steps.
